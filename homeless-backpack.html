Homeless backpack

Twin cities homeless survival backpack fund

My name is Mahad Omar and I'm raising funds to buy backpacks with basic necessities for those experiencing homelessness in Minneapolis-St. Paul. Donation purchases will be posted to a public google drive for transparency, link on the bottom.

The homeless community is especially vulnerable during these cold months. They are in need of basic necessities for survival and access to a means of helping themselves to stay alive.

What's in each backpack:

  • Gloves, scarf, socks, winter hat Mask pack
  • Non-persishable food items
  • Eating utensils, can opener
  • Duct tape, pen, pad
  • List of resources/contacts
  • Menstrual products for those requesting it
  • Bottled water
  • Hand warmer packets
  • Emergency blanket
  • Toiletry pack
  • Laundry Soap
